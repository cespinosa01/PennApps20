About

Zero Waste: UI/UX Case Study

Created during PennApps 2019 by Cindy Espinosa and Malini Kundamal
under route for social good.

Problem: Food goes to waste in fridges daily.

Solution: An AI generated recipe tool that will maximize
how a person uses their food items in order to reduce waste.
There are more possibilities with this solution, because future iterations could
predict food spoiling based on expiration dates, climate, and geographic location.

Tools used for backend: python, amazon web services, machine learning, github
Tools used for frontend: Adobe Xd, UIkit, HTML5, CSS, github

Competitive Research: We found that in the United States, about 4.2 trillion gallons of water
are wasted by not consuming @5% of food according to PLOS One as reported by LA Times.
