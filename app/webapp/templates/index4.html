faq

why don't expiration labels work?

According to Waste Management 84% of people waste food according to the printed "expiration date" on items, which are not USDA approved.

"use by" vs. "best by" vs. "sell by"? According to Times

  • "Use by" means it is the last day of prime quality
  • "Best by" means prime taste quality
  • "Sell by" marks for the store how long the item should be displayed

are there current actions being taken?

Yes, according to the organization Move for Hunger large non-profits and corporate organiztaions have pledged to cut back half of food waste in 11 years.
